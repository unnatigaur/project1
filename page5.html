About

We welcome you to participate in ‘Dekho Apna Desh, People’s Choice 2024’ a tourist destination poll by the Ministry of Tourism Participants can input their favorite tourist attractions into five defined categories that align with their preferences. It's mandatory for participants to select at least one attraction they have already visited from any of the five categories and choose at least one attraction that they wish to visit in the future. The winning attractions identified will enable the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India to develop attractions and destinations in mission mode, contributing to India’s journey towards Viksit Bharat@2047

Tourism in India

Spiritual

Temples, Churches, Monasteries, Mosques, Pilgrimages, Circuits (multi-attraction route), or any religious site.

Cultural & Heritage

IForts, Monuments, Statues, Palaces, Museums, Memorials, Caves, Intangible Cultural Heritage, Circuits (multi-attraction route), or any heritage site

Nature & Wildlife

Beaches, Lakes, Waterfalls, Rivers, Hill Stations, National Parks, Sanctuaries, Reserves, Zoos, Islands, Circuits (multi-attraction route), or any natural site

Adventure

Sites for Trekking, Hiking, Rafting, Snorkeling, Kayaking, Circuits (multi-attraction route) or any other adventure activity

Others

Any other tourist attraction, Vibrant Villages, Destinations for Wellness, Medical, Conferences Tourism or Circuits (multi-attraction route)

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Nationality and Residency: Voting is open for individuals residing in and outside of India
  2. Registration: Participants residing in India must have an Indian Mobile Number Participants residing outside of India must have an Email-ID
  3. Participation Limit: Participants can vote only once per mobile number and/or email ID

    4. General Terms & Conditions

    1. Participants must not provide any false information in the registration process.
    2. Participants must keep their contact information accurate and up-to-date.
    3. The participants will not use this voting initiative to do anything unlawful, misleading, malicious, or discriminatory.
    4. If any participant is determined to have violated the terms of the contest, the Ministry will have all rights to disqualify the participant without prior notice.
    5. If required, the Ministry may change the terms and conditions.
    6. The Ministry decision is final and cannot be challenged.
    7. The Ministry reserve the right in their sole discretion to withdraw participation of any individual or reject any submission at any point of time during the process.
